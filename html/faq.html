FAQs


How to order?

  • Browse our online shop and select the items you like to purchase
  • Add the items to your cart and proceed to checkout
  • Provide your shipping and contact information
  • Select your preferred mode of payment (GCash or Palawan)
  • Review your order details and confirm purchase

What courier do you use when shipping within the country?

We use J&T Express as our courier for deliveries within the Philippines.


How many days will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times may vary depending on your location, but we strive to deliver orders within 3-5 business days from the date of purchase


How much is the shipping fee?

Shipping fees may vary depending on your location, quantity and weight of your purchase. For orders within the Philippines, our standard shipping fee is Php 150. However, for larger orders or deliveries to certain remote areas, additional shipping charges may apply. We will provide you with a total cost estimate, including shipping fees, at checkout before you confirm your purchase.


Where's the size chart?

You can find our size chart in the navigation bar of our website. Simply click on the "Size Chart" tab to view our sizing guide and find the perfect fit for your purchase.


What are the modes of payment?

We accept payments through Gcash or Palawan Express. Please note that we do not accept cash on delivery (COD) at this time.


What is your Return & Exchange Policy?

At PLEYN Clothing, we want our customers to be completely satisfied with their purchase. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 3 days of receiving the item and we will work with you to resolve the issue. We do not offer returns or exchanges for items that do not fit, so please check our size chart carefully before placing your order.

If you need to return a damaged or defective item, we will provide you with instructions on how to send it back to us. Once we receive the item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund or exchange. Please note that we do not refund shipping costs and the customer is responsible for any return shipping fees. Returns must be sent back to us within 7 days of our authorization, and the item must be in its original packaging with all tags attached. If you have any questions about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
